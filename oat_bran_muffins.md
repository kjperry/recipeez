# Oat Bran Muffins

## Ingredients
* 1.5 c. oat bran
* 1.5 c. all purpose flour
* .5 c. brown sugar
* 2 tsp baking powder
* 2 tsp baking soda
* .5 tsp salt
* 1 c. applesauce
* 2 large eggs
* .25 c. vegetable oil

## Steps
1. Makes 12 muffins
2. Preheat to 400
3. Mix bran, flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, applesauce, eggs, and oil
4. Grease muffin tin
5. Bake ~15 minutes (test with toothpick)
