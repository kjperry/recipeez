# broth

## Ingredients
* bones, carcass, etc.
* Water

## Steps
1. Gather bones, carcass, etc.
1. If using big bones, like ribs, consider giving them an additional roast in the oven, and/or whacking them with a hammer to release even more... bone stuff.
1. Put stuff into instant pot and cover with water.
1. Pressure cook on high for 2 hours. Quick release, but it doesn't really matter.
1. Strain through cheesecloth and use or jar.
