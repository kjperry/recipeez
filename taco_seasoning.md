# Taco seasoning

# Ingredients
* 1 tbsp chili powder
* 1 tbsp chipotle powder
* 1 tbsp cumin
* 2 tsp black pepper
* 1 tsp onion powder
* 1 tsp oregano
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp garlic powder
* 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
* Smidge cayenne

## Steps
1. Mix.
1. 2 tbsp per pound of ground beef for tacos.
